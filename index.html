SuperZekes's PvP Pack

This modpack is perfect for PvPers. It includes mods such as small totem pops to improve visibility during battles, world host to easily host worlds for PvP with friends, and various quality of life features. It also supports all versions of Minecraft. Check out some screenshots here.

Looking for a place to PvP?

I would recommend PvP Legacy for the best 1.9 PvP experience.

For 1.8 PvP, I highly recommend Hypixel.
